Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

Hidden Face ("the App") is designed to apply visual filters and face anonymization effects to photos and videos. The App processes media locally on your device and does not require account creation.

2. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No user accounts
  • No facial data storage
  • No biometric data collection
  • No cloud uploads
  • No tracking

All image and video processing is performed locally on the user's device.

3. Photo and Video Processing

The App allows users to load photos and videos (up to 3 minutes in length) from their device.

Face detection and filter application are performed entirely offline.

4. Anonymization and Similarity Score

The App includes a visual analyzer that estimates similarity between the original and processed image. This score is approximate and provided for informational purposes only.

Results may vary depending on lighting, angle, image quality, and evolving technologies.

5. Accuracy Disclaimer

Face masking and filter placement may not always be perfectly aligned. The similarity score may not reflect real-world AI recognition accuracy.

The App does not guarantee complete anonymity, protection, or resistance against facial recognition systems.

Technology evolves rapidly, and effectiveness may change over time.

  • Failure to prevent recognition by third-party systems
  • Any consequences resulting from the use of the App

Users assume full responsibility for how they use the App and any content processed with it.

7. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external data processors.

8. Children's Privacy
